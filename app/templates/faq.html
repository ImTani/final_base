Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: What is the Waste Pickup Tracker web app?
A: The Waste Pickup Tracker web app is a platform designed to help households keep track of waste collection schedules and receive alerts when the waste picker is nearby. It aims to assist users in better preparation for waste disposal.

Usage and Alerts

Q: How do I receive alerts for waste pickups?
A: Ensure that you have enabled notifications in your account settings. You will receive alerts based on the waste pickup schedule and when the waste picker is nearby.
