Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the Refuse Reapers?

A: Refuse Reapers is a platform designed to help households keep track of waste collection schedules and receive alerts when the waste picker is nearby. It aims to assist users in better preparation for waste disposal.

Q: How do I sign up for an account?

A: To sign up for an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the app's homepage. Follow the on-screen instructions to create your account by providing the required information.

Q: Is there a cost to use the service?

A: No. Refuse Reapers is entirely free and the service is closely tied with the system that the government and the Municipal Corporations have created which allows the sustainability of our system.

Q: How can I reset my password?

A: To reset your password, navigate to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to set a new password.

Q: Can I delete my account?

A: Yes, you can delete your account. Log in to your account, go to the account settings, and find the option to delete your account. Follow the provided instructions to complete the account deletion process.

Q: How do I receive alerts for waste pickups?

A: Ensure that you have enabled notifications in your account settings. You will receive alerts based on the waste pickup schedule and when the waste picker is nearby.

Q: Can I customize the types of alerts I receive?

A: Yes, you can customize your notification preferences in the account settings. Choose the types of alerts you want to receive, such as scheduled pickup alerts or proximity alerts.

Q: What happens if I miss a waste pickup?

A: If you miss a waste pickup, check the app for any rescheduling information. Additionally, review your notification settings to ensure you are receiving timely alerts.

Q: I'm experiencing technical issues. How can I get help?

A: For technical support, please contact us by pressing the Contact Us button on the homepage. Provide details about the issue you're facing, and our team will assist you as soon as possible.

Q: Is the app available on mobile devices?

A: Although a dedicated app is not available on mobile devices, users can still use their mobile devices to access the website, as the website is fully responsive with all devices.
