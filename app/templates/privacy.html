Privacy Policy

Last updated: [3/2/2024]

Introduction

Welcome to the Waste Pickup Tracker web app! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  • Username: Used for account identification.
  • Email Address: Used for communication and account recovery.
  • Password: Securely stored for account authentication.
  • Location: User's home address for routing purposes.
  • Phone Number: Optional contact information for notifications.

Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Truck and User Data: Information related to waste pickup schedules, truck routes, and user preferences.
  • App Usage: Log of interactions, alerts received, and other activities within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • User Authentication: To verify your identity when you log in.
  • Waste Pickup Services: To provide accurate waste pickup schedules and alerts.
  • Communication: To send notifications, updates, and respond to inquiries.
  • Improvement of Services: To enhance and customize your experience with the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. You will be notified of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
